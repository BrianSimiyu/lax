OUR STORY

LAX Bar is a relaxed meeting place on Yryönkatu street, founded by two friends, Brian and Jay. The interior of the bar has been designed and implemented by top Finnish designers, giving the bar a relaxed atmosphere while maintaining a Scandinavian design concept. We specialize in wines and coktails.
