OUR STORY

The stern chamber is a relaxed meeting place on Eerikinkatu, founded by sisters, Jonna and Jenna. The interior has been designed and implemented as much as possible by sisters and family members. The project began in Ostrobothnia with the dismantling of a barn, from where the bar got boards for the chamber wall and bar counter. Genuine and life-seeing materials stylishly bring warmth to this living room for every man and woman.
